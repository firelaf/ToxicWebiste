Our Story

The Ragtag Team

Toxic was established in the summer of 2016 by our founder Svet. Him, along with Kris, Vasko, Niki, Miro, Ivo, and Kiro teamed up to try out for a spot at the asian pop culture event 'Aniventure 2016's k-pop dance cover panel panel. The 7 started off as just a bunch of buddies looking for a hobby and they could have never known what the future held. We were the first all male k-pop cover dance group in Bulgaria and we remain the only one to this day.

Learning Curve

Our debut performance was less than spectacular to say the least. But we held on and kept on going until eventually something started clicking. We developed synergy and synchronicity over our next few performances. With our new member Dan, we headed back to the stage for a charity concert a few months after our debut, and we felt driven to make something from this.

Competition

The culmination of our efforts in our first year together was us finally competiting on stage. We went up against the best k-pop dancers in Bulgaria at the 'K-Pop World Festival: Sofia'. We wanted to prove to ourselves and the rest of the community that the all-male group is not just a gimmick and we showed them our best with our performance of 'Infinite - The Eye'. We took home third place, a result with which we were extremely happy with. It served only to increase our drive for performance and competition in the future.

Ambition

As our vigor and drive crept up, so did our desire to do something big. What better way to impress than return to the stage it all started on? Armed with our new member Kircho, we headed out for our most ambitious performance yet, on the biggest stage. It was time for 'Aniventure 2017', and our 9-man performance of 'SF9 - K.O.'.

Success

Fast forward to the next year. We return as five members to 'K-Pop World Festival: Sofia' in 2018 armed with two years of experience, three months of practice, and determination to win. A third place finish wouldn't satisfy us anymore. We aimed to win it all. With our most intense choreography yet, 'A.C.E - Callin', we convincingly snatched the gold medal, and the feeling was indescribable. The biggest competition in our field was finally ours.
As winners of the regional round of 'K-Pop World Festival', we entered the internal competition. 12 teams from around the world were selected to head out all the way to Changwon, South Korea, train for two weeks, and compete in the Festival's finals. All broadcasted on Korea's national TV, KBS. And we did it once again, being the first Bulgarian group to ever compete in the finals of the festival.

Beyond Korea

Since our adventure to Korea, we've been hard at work developing ourselves and our craft, and having as much fun as we can doing it. Pandemic times have hit us hard, but with our new addition, Kai, we can't wait to go back on stage. Our story is far from over, and we hope you stay along, and enjoy it with us.
